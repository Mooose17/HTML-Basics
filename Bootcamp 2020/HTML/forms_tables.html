Forms and Tables

What are tables?

  • A way to move data around
  • Developers liked to use them in the early days of WEBDES make a bunch of cells and take up dead space inbetween cells.
  • Tables should be used to display tabular data.
  • Use Tabels to display data that can be displayed in tabular format.
  • Quite a few elements can co in a table.
